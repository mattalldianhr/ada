Creative Strategy Review

Five distinct advertising approaches for Ada, each grounded in proven frameworks from advertising's golden age. Four executions per strategy.

Guiding Principles (Classic Agency Wisdom)

  • One Big Idea per campaign, expressed simply
  • Benefits over features - what does the user GET?
  • Specificity builds credibility - avoid vague claims
  • Respect audience intelligence - no hard sell
  • Find the human truth - what tension does Ada resolve?
  • Position against alternatives - what makes us different?

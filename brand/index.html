Brand Overview

Ada is a voice-driven content editor for writers who think out loud. Our brand is warm, intelligent, and approachable.

01

Direct but warm

Like talking to a smart friend over coffee. Confident without being cold or corporate.

02

Intelligent but unpretentious

We use technical terms naturally, never condescending. We respect our users' intelligence.

03

Paper-like warmth

Warm off-white backgrounds, amber accents, and thoughtful typography create a crafted feel.

Typography

Two fonts: Inter for UI, Literata for content. Following Butterick's Practical Typography principles.

'Inter', -apple-system, system-ui, sans-serif
Used for buttons, labels, navigation, and all UI elements.

Content Font
Literata

'Literata', 'Source Serif Pro', Georgia, serif
Used for document content, brand moments, and editorial text.

Monospace
ui-monospace

ui-monospace, SFMono-Regular, Menlo, Monaco, Consolas, monospace

Voice & Tone

Direct but warm. Like talking to a smart friend over coffee.

Do

"Read the docs before you start. It'll save you time."
"Use what you have. It's faster."
"Accept, dismiss, or talk it through."

Don't

"It is recommended that users familiarize themselves with the documentation."
"Leverage existing resources to optimize efficiency."
"The system will process your request accordingly."

Key Phrases

"Edit with your voice."
"A second set of eyes. Always."
"Writers who think out loud."
"Your voice, amplified."
"Local transcription, cloud intelligence."

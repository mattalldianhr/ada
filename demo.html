05

Voice Guide

Point Ada to your style guide. It learns your tone and ensures consistency across all suggestions.

VOICE.md Style Guide 
# Voice Guide

## Tone
Confident but not arrogant. Direct but warm.
Like talking to a smart friend over coffee.

## Style
- Short paragraphs (2-3 sentences max)
- Active voice always
- No jargon unless absolutely necessary
- Start with the point, then explain

## Avoid
- Corporate speak ("leverage", "synergy")
- Hedging language ("I think", "maybe")
- Passive constructions
- Long, complex sentences

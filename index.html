Two modes. One voice.

Socratic Mode

Highlight text, press a hotkey, and discuss it. Ask questions like "Is this too formal?" or "Make this more concrete." Have a conversation, then apply the refined version.

⌘ + ⇧ + S

Didactic Mode

Highlight text, press a hotkey, and tell Ada what you want. Speak the replacement directly. Ada transcribes, cleans up filler words, and shows a diff.

⌘ + ⇧ + D

Local & Private

Speech recognition runs entirely on your Mac. Your drafts never leave your machine. Only the text you're actively editing is sent to the AI for suggestions.

Comment Threads

Create multiple annotations on different passages. Each becomes a persistent conversation thread in the margin, just like comments in Google Docs, but voice-first.

Phase 1

Product Foundation

  • Core editing engine with TipTap

    Rich text editor with voice-driven editing, highlight-to-speak workflow, and real-time streaming responses from Claude.

    Product Shipping in v0.1.9

  • Socratic Mode

    Conversational refinement through voice. Highlight text, speak your thought, get a nuanced suggestion with the reasoning behind it. Uses <find>/<replace> diffs for surgical edits.

    Product Cmd+Shift+S

  • Dictation Mode

    Direct spoken text replacement with light grammar cleanup. Say what you mean, it replaces the selection. Minimal AI intervention.

    Product Cmd+Shift+D

  • Local Whisper transcription

    Browser-based speech recognition via Transformers.js. No audio leaves the device. ~40MB model cached in browser. Silence detection auto-stops after 4s.

    Product Xenova/whisper-tiny.en

  • AI Agents system

    Background monitors that watch as you write and surface suggestions. Custom rules, multiple agents running simultaneously, non-intrusive sidenote annotations.

    Product Live in app

  • Multi-annotation system

    Multiple conversations in the sidebar simultaneously. Accept, reject, revert edits. Color-coded highlights cycle through 5 colors. Full conversation history per annotation.

    Product

  • Voice guide & context file support

    VOICE.md for tone/style rules, context files for project-specific knowledge. Relevance-scored and injected into AI prompts.

    Product

  • Apple code signing & notarization

    Signed with Developer ID, notarized by Apple, stapled DMGs for both Apple Silicon and Intel. No Gatekeeper warnings on install.

    Product Notarized Feb 2, 2026

  • E2E test suite with Playwright

    Automated tests for UI, editor behavior, keyboard shortcuts, and voice pipeline. TTS-generated test audio clips for voice mode testing.

    Product

  • Sentry usage tracking

    Error monitoring and usage analytics integrated. Tracking key events across the app.

    Product Added in v0.1.7

Phase 4 — Now

Launch Preparation

  • Demo video storyboard

    5-act structure, 60-90 seconds. Shot list with sample content, transitions, music direction, and 30-second cut variant. Ready for production.

    Launch VIDEO_STORYBOARD.md

  • Product Hunt demo recording guide

    3-minute demo structure: app launch, UI overview, Socratic mode, accept/reject flow, Dictation mode, agents. Narration cues and post-production notes included.

    Launch DEMO_RECORDING.md

  • Define the narrative position

    "The thinking is the writing." Intentionality over automation. Every piece of launch content—ads, posts, the maker's comment—should show a writer having a thought about their own text, speaking it, getting a suggestion, and deciding. The philosophy is the structure, not a tagline bolted on top.

    Launch brand/why.html

  • Record Product Hunt demo video

    Execute the storyboard. Screen capture with voiceover. The demo itself should embody the loop: notice a problem, speak a thought, get a suggestion, decide. Speed up AI waits (2x-4x). Background music, captions. 1080p.

    Launch

  • Write Product Hunt listing

    Maker's first comment should tell the origin story: "I watched autonomous agents go viral and felt something was off." Lead with the two-questions framework, then let the product speak for itself. Tagline, gallery (5-8 screenshots), description. Tuesday/Wednesday launch, 12:01 AM PST.

    Launch

  • Prepare LinkedIn launch series

    Three posts across launch week. Post 1: the position—"Everyone's building AI that acts on your behalf. I built one that amplifies your intentional action." Post 2: the co-creation story—product, website, 102 ads, all built through human-AI dialogue with the human steering. Post 3: the demo, with the writing-pain hook. Demo GIF or short video in each.

    Launch

  • Produce Instagram/Reel ad creatives

    Every ad follows the same structure: writer notices a problem in their text, speaks their thought, gets a suggestion, decides. The hook varies (jargon, cringe email, hedging, specificity) but the loop is always visible. Screen record from brand/ads.html at proper aspect ratios. 15-30s Reels + 5-10s Stories.

    Launch

  • Set up Meta ad account & campaigns

    Create Business Manager, ad account, pixel. Upload creatives. Targeting: writers, content creators, marketers, 25-45. A/B test hooks but keep the underlying structure consistent across all variants.

    Launch

Phase 6

Monetization

Implementation plan documented (42 tasks, 8 priority levels). Ship after validating demand from launch.

Pricing strategy defined

Free trial, then one-time purchase for BYOK users (the value is the tool, not the API), subscription tiers for managed API access. Starter ($5/mo, higher limits), Pro ($19/mo, unlimited).

Implementation plan written

42 tasks across 8 priority levels: feature flags, entitlements, onboarding modal, account menu, usage display, upgrade modal, agent limits, integration polish.

Feature flags & entitlements

Server-side toggles for auth/billing/agents. Plan limit enforcement (maxAgents per tier).

Auth & onboarding

OAuth flow (Google/GitHub) with "use own API key" fallback. Onboarding modal for new users.

Stripe integration

Checkout sessions, subscription management, usage metering. Upgrade prompts when hitting limits.

Usage dashboard

Daily/monthly request bars in settings. Clear visibility into what you've used and what's left.
